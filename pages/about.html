   Welcome to Swarn Swapn GruhUdyog, where we bring the authentic taste of homemade noodles(Shevai) to your table .

स्वर्णस्वप्न गृहउद्योग   At Swarn Swapn GruhUdyog , we have a passion for preserving traditional culinary practices while infusing them with modern flair. Our journey began in May 2011, when Mrs. Sangita Sudhakar Satpute embarked on a quest to recreate the flavors of their childhood memories. Inspired by the rich heritage of homemade noodles(Shevai ), we set out to share this cherished tradition with Shevai lovers everywhere.

  What sets us apart is our commitment to quality and authenticity. We carefully select the finest ingredients, sourcing locally whenever possible, to ensure every batch of noodles is crafted with love and attention to detail. Each strand carries the essence of homemade goodness, bringing warmth and comfort to every meal.

  But our story doesn't end with the noodles themselves. We believe in fostering a sense of community around the dining table. Whether you're enjoying a bowl with family or sharing stories with friends, our noodles are more than just food—they're a catalyst for connection and shared experiences.

  From our kitchen to yours, we invite you to savor the simple pleasure of homemade noodles. Join us on this culinary journey, where every bite tells a story and every meal is a celebration of tradition and togetherness.

  Thank you for choosing Swarn Swapn GruhUdyog. Let's make every moment around the table memorable.
